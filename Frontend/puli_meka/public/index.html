How to Play:

  • Goats (20): Place one per turn until all are placed, then move orthogonally or diagonally
  • Tigers (4): Move orthogonally or diagonally, can capture goats by jumping over them
  • Goats win: By blocking all tigers from moving
  • Tigers win: By capturing enough goats (reduce to 5 or fewer)
